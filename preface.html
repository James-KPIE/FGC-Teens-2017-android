Preface

It’s so thrilling to be alive! It’s not just about being alive, but that we are alive as significant participators in God’s purpose on earth. He counted us worthy to be labourers in His vineyard and vessels of His glory. Every new day is an indication that God has not finished with us yet, we are not yet done on this side of eternity. There’s still so much to be done, there’s a lot in store for us to accomplish as children and messengers of God.

However, we can hardly accomplish anything without Him. It’s “not by might nor by power, but by my Spirit,” says the LORD Almighty (Zech. 4:6). Certainly, if we are going to journey through 2017 successfully, we can’t go it only. We can’t afford to do things our way. We need the Holy Spirit to lead the way. We need to keep in step with Him, so He can carry us along on this trip of faith.

Our theme for the Teens manual, ‘Dwelling in the Realm of the Holy Spirit’, provides a pertinent roadmap for us to chart the path our purpose takes us in the New Year. We can’t get on the road trying to figure out things by ourselves, or just go with the flow. That is not the way we roll! That is the way of the world. But we belong to a different realm. You, however, are not in the realm of the flesh but are in the realm of the Spirit, if indeed the Spirit of God lives in you… (Romans 8:9, NIV)

Yes indeed! We belong in the realm of the Spirit. As we explore the manual, prayerfully, faithfully and with great expectation, we are going to discover timely truths that will motivate us to walk with the Spirit, as we are being transformed from one level of glory to another, to be conformed to the image of Christ.

What do you need to take along with you, as you join this expedition with the Spirit? All that is required is an open mind and a willing heart.

Come with me on this adventure with the Spirit!

Rev. (Mrs.) K. O. Ayanbanjo
The Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria
