Lesson 39

NATIONAL SUNDAY SCHOOL PROMOTION WEEK

Lesson Starts

National Sunday School Promotion Day This is the last rally for the year. The importance of this rally is that it evaluates the overall performance of Sunday school for the year. With it the examination and promotion of students to the next class are overviewed. Program for the rally should be well thought, planned and executed. This rally is unlike its previous ones is not a day program but a week long one to be climaxed on Sunday. Among others there should be:

1. Tuesday/Wednesday: The Bible Study teaching of the week should be on theme of the rally

2. Friday: Departmental Debate/Quiz Competition with award

3. Saturday: Teachers and their students should outreach their community, possibly with flyers inviting people to the Sunday rally

4. Sunday: A short message with the Sunday school promotion will be appropriate from a seasoned minister, after the presentations from various classes and departments. Other programs for the day include:

a. Recitations

b. Talkbacks

c. Playlets

d. Drama

5. Evaluation of the general Sunday school operation and performance.

6. Recognition and appreciation of the best class, student, teacher and workers
