Lesson 14

QUARTERLY SUNDAY SCHOOL RALLY

Lesson Starts

It is generally agreed that a meaningful Sunday school cannot and should not be less than 45 minutes, for there to be tangible impartation. Within this Sunday school time for rally, all departments and classes are expected to make presentations centering around the themes and topics of the last 13 weeks lessons. The importance of Sunday school rally is too review, refresh and evaluate for further progress. Programme for the rally should be well thought, planned and executed. Among other there should be:

  1. Students should be encouraged to invite their friends to the rally.
  1. The various classes should have prepared for their various presentations such as:

a. Recitations

b. Talkbacks from the previous twelve lessons

c. Playlets from lessons

  1. Evaluation of the Sunday school operation within the last three months.
  1. Recognition and appreciation of the best class, student and teacher.
  1. The message of the day can be geared toward the importance of the Word. (Optional)
