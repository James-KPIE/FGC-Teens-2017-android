Forward

Apostle Paul enjoined the Church in Galatia , “This I say then, Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfill the lust of the flesh… If we live in the Spirit, let us also walk in the Spirit” (Galatians 5:16; 25). The Holy Spirit is very significant in the life of a believer. Through Him we obtained the conviction that led to our conversion. Through Him we obtained the grace and power to declare our faith to others – preaching the Gospel. Through Him we live a daily life of consecration and committed service in God’s vineyard while we look forward with hope to the glorious second appearing of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ. Through Him we are sealed unto the day of redemption. Understanding the peculiar and precious role of the Holy Spirit in our affairs as believers, it behoves us to make conscious effort and conduct our lives in accordance with the dictates of the Holy Spirit.

The Person, Gifts, Fruit and Power of the Holy Spirit among others are important understanding you must have as teenagers as you seek to grow in the knowledge of God and attain the fulness of Christ.

Particularly, as teenagers, dwelling in the realm of the Holy Spirit opens you up to unquantifiable opportunities and blessings. This edition of the Teens Manual for our Sunday School has, therefore, been prepared to deepen our understanding and knowledge in this realm with the hope that we would all use it carefully, consciously and prayerfully.

Beloved, let us give our devoted attention to it and allow the Holy Spirit to minister to you through it. I appreciate everyone who has been part of its preparation. May God abundantly reward you all in Jesus name.

Shalom and Maranatha.

Rev. Felix Meduoye,
General Overseer,
The Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria
